Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix bread crumbs with milk to make dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and fry until golden.
  3. Soak in warm sugar syrup for 2 hours.

Barfi Recipe

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Cook milk powder, ghee, and sugar until thick.
  2. Spread onto a greased tray and let it set.
  3. Cut into squares and serve.
